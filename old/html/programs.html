The Autism Generator

You can download it by clicking here.

It's a program that fetches 4 pictures of people from thispersondoesnotexist.com, and if you click one there's a 1 in 4 chance he or she has autism, and you'll lose. If not, you'll earn a point in the textbox and you can try again.

Why does it exist? I don't know, i wanted to learn C# and i don't like being serious. It was very educational making this program, so that's good! :D

Why does it take so long (actually 3006ms longer than usual) to load the pictures? Because i set a delay of 1002ms between all photos, because the website i fetch them from produces a new image every 1000ms and otherwise duplicates would appear. Who would like that now.

If you want to see the code, here's the github repository. Do what you like with it, i don't really care as long as i don't lose any of my files.
